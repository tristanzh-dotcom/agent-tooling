Copyright of the original visual designs, layouts, illustrations and decorative

## Permitted use

- Personal study and research — e.g. exploring how PPT layouts are built,
  experimenting with AI-driven slide generation, prototyping inside a single
  person's workspace.
- Educational discussion in non-commercial contexts.

## Prohibited use

- **Commercial use of any kind**, including but not limited to:
  - Commercial presentations, sales decks, pitch decks for paid services
  - Investor relations / corporate financial communication
  - Paid training material redistribution
  - Customer-facing proposals from a for-profit entity
  - Internal corporate use in any organization whose primary business is
    for-profit
  - Resale, repackaging, or rehosting of the templates as a paid product
  - Any redistribution of the templates as part of a paid SaaS / API / plugin
- Removing or modifying this notice
- Removing template-author attribution wherever it appears inside the original
  PPTX files

## How to obtain commercial rights

If you wish to use any of the templates commercially, you must contact the
**original template author** (usually visible inside the source PPTX file's
"作者 / 设计师" metadata or the original distribution page on 稻壳儿 / WPS)
and obtain explicit written authorization.

The maintainer of this repository (GordenSun) does **not** hold commercial
licensing rights to the templates and cannot grant them.
